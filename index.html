Wangari Maathai

Nobel Peace Prize Winning Environmentalist
Wangari in 2006

Wangari Maathai was an environmental and political activist and Nobel Laureate. She founded the Green Belt Movement in 1977 focussed on environmental conservation. In 2004 she became the first African woman to receive a Nobel Peace Prize for her contribution to sustainable development, democracy and peace.
